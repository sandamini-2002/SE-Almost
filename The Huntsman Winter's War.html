DIRECTOR

Cedric Nicolas-Troyan

CAST

Chris Hemsworth, Jessica Chastain, Charlize Theron, Emily Blunt, Sheridan Smith, Alexandra Roach, and Rob Brydon

SUMMARY

The Huntsman: Winter's War is a prequel and sequel to Snow White and the Huntsman that tells the story of Eric and Sara, who are raised in the army of the Ice Queen, Freya. The film follows the two as they try to hide their forbidden love while fighting to survive the intentions of Freya and her sister, Ravenna.
